House Information

Our chapter house is located at 1234 Fraternity Row, Urbana, IL. It serves as a hub for our activities, meetings, and social events. The house features comfortable living spaces, study areas, and recreational facilities.

If you are interested in living in the house, please contact our House Manager at house@zbtrho.com.
