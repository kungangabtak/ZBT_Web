History

The Zeta Beta Tau (ZBT) fraternity was founded in 1898 as the world's first Jewish fraternity. Since then, it has grown to include members of all backgrounds, promoting brotherhood, leadership, and community service.

The RHO chapter at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign was established in 1921, becoming a vibrant part of the campus community. Over the years, the RHO chapter has been involved in numerous philanthropic events, academic initiatives, and social activities that contribute to the personal growth of its members.

With a strong emphasis on leadership and academic excellence, the RHO chapter has produced many distinguished alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields.
